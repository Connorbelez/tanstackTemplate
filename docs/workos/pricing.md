## Frequently asked questions
What's a connection?
A connection represents the relationship between WorkOS and any group of end users. Each enterprise customer you support with SSO or Directory Sync is counted as one connection.
Does the number of end users affect pricing?
No, each connection at WorkOS is billed the same, regardless of the identity provider (IdP), directory service used, or the total number of end users.
Can I try WorkOS before paying for it?
WorkOS is free to get started and you don't need to add a credit card until you are ready to move to production.
What's the difference between Staging and Production?
The staging environment is made for testing your integrations, while the production environment is built for live applications that use real customer data. All WorkOS products are available in staging to try at no cost.
Can I use Admin Portal without customizing it?
Yes, Admin Portal is fully functional without customization and is included in all WorkOS accounts. Custom branding and custom domains can be applied for an additional monthly fee.
How do you count the 1 million free users?
WorkOS User Management is free for up to 1 million monthly active users. A user is considered active if they perform any user action (for example: sign up, sign in, or profile update) within the calendar month. Contact us to learn more about volume pricing.
