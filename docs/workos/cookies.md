# WorkOS Cookie Policy  

This is the Cookie Policy for WorkOS, accessible from workos.com  

* * *
## How We Use Cookies
We use cookies for a variety of reasons as detailed below. It is recommended that you leave on all cookies as they are used to improve our service.
## Disabling Cookies
You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings in your browser (see your browser Help for how to do this). You can also prevent or allow the setting of cookies in our dashboard. Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of this site.  

## The Cookies We Set  

  * Login related cookies  

We use cookies when you are logged in so that we can maintain your logged-in state. This prevents you from having to log in every single time you visit a new page. These cookies are removed or invalidated when you log out to ensure that users can only access certain features when logged in.
## Third Party Cookies  

In some cases we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. This section details which third party cookies you will encounter on this site.
  * Third party analytics are used to track and measure usage of this site so that we can continue to produce engaging content. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site or pages you visit which helps us to understand how we can improve the site for you.  
‍  
For more information on Google Analytics cookies, see the [official Google privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).  
‍  
For more information on Segment cookies, see the [official Segment privacy policy](https://segment.com/legal/privacy/).  

  * Third party Live chat cookies are used to help us offer you support. These cookies help us identify you to continue offering support after you go offline.  
  
For more information on ZenDesk cookies, see the [official ZenDesk privacy policy](https://www.zendesk.com/company/privacy-and-data-protection/).  

## More Information
However, if you are still looking for more information, then you can contact us through one of these methods:
