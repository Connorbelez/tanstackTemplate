# Careers at WorkOS
We build tools for developers that unlock equal access to the enterprise market. Our vision is to give every software company the ability to have their products used by the biggest enterprises in the world.  
‍  
Join our rapidly expanding team that prioritizes transparency, developer-led initiatives, and iterative processes to build high-quality products.
[View open positions](https://workos.com/careers#open-positions)
![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/621f54116cab10f6e9215d8b/624d8a8000d7ffa6ca1db8a3_careers-map.png)
## Our benefits
We aim to provide generous benefits to our team. The following benefits are for US employees; we provide comparable benefits outside the US.
### Medical, dental, and vision
WorkOS pays 90% of premiums for employees and dependents to help keep your family healthy.
### Retirement & 401k
WorkOS provides a 3% match of your 401k contributions to help you save for retirement.
### Vacation & leave
Relax and recharge with 20 days of paid yearly vacation. We also offer 12 weeks of parental leave for new parents.
### Fitness
Monthly stipend for gyms, yoga classes, race registrations, or whatever keeps you active.
### Wellness
Monthly stipend for a massage, meditation class, therapy, or activities that enhance your mental well-being.
### Home office
Outfit your working space with a stipend for equipment that will keep you comfortable and productive.
## Open positions
WorkOS is an equal opportunity employer and we will consider all qualified applicants without regard to race, color, nationality, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, religion, disability or age.
